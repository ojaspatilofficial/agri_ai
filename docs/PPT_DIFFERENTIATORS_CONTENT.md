## 3) How We Differ from Traditional Farming (Slide 3)

### Traditional Farming (Ground Reality)
- Decisions mostly based on experience, not live data
- Irrigation/fertilizer often scheduled by routine, not crop condition
- Disease response is reactive (after visible damage)
- Market selling is often distress-based, not forecast-based
- Sustainability actions are not measured or rewarded

### Agri AI Approach
- Data + AI based recommendations (soil, weather, water, fertilizer, disease, yield, market)
- Conflict-aware decisions (example: postpone irrigation if rain is likely)
- Early risk visibility (disease + climate risk)
- Price forecast + best selling window guidance
- Green practices tracked and rewarded through tokenized system

---

## 4) Core Product Differentiators (Slide 4)
- **Multi-Agent Intelligence (17 agents):** Specialized AI modules instead of one generic advisory engine  
- **End-to-End Journey Coverage:** From sowing decisions to selling strategy  
- **Multilingual + Voice-first UX:** English, Hindi, Marathi with voice interaction  
- **Farmer + Admin Loop:** Action logs, escalation, approval workflow, and callback support  
- **Sustainability with Incentives:** Blockchain-based transparent reward history for eco-friendly actions  
- **Integrated Risk Layer:** Weather + climate + crop-health + market together, not in silos

## 6) Positioning Against JioKrishi / Similar Platforms (Slide 6)
> Use this slide as **competitive positioning**, not a negative comparison.

### What similar platforms generally do well
- Good digital access to advisories and farm information
- Useful ecosystem presence and farmer outreach
- Inputs/market linkage in selected workflows

### Where Agri AI is positioned differently
- **Deeper orchestration:** Multiple specialized agents with lead-agent coordination logic
- **Conflict resolution logic:** Decisions are reconciled across weather, water, fertilizer, and disease recommendations
- **Actionability focus:** Not just data display — prioritized, practical next-step suggestions
- **Voice + multilingual execution layer:** Better for low-text and first-time digital users
- **Sustainability accountability:** Reward trail with transparent logging mindset
- **Farmer-support operations layer:** Admin queue, approvals, call scheduling flow
